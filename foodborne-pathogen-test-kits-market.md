<p><p>The foodborne pathogen test kits market features various types, primarily focusing on PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). PCR tests rapidly detect and amplify specific DNA sequences from pathogens, offering high sensitivity and speed for identifying contamination in food samples. In contrast, ELISA tests utilize antibodies to identify and quantify proteins related to pathogens, providing reliable results in a variety of food matrices. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>

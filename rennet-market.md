<p><p>Rennet is an essential enzyme in cheese production, and it comes in various types. Vegetable rennet is derived from plants, such as thistle or nettle, appealing to vegetarian consumers. Microbial rennet, sourced from fungi or bacteria, offers a cost-effective and consistent alternative to animal rennet. Genetically modified rennet involves using genetically engineered organisms to produce chymosin, the key enzyme in traditional rennet, providing high efficiency and reliability. Each type caters to specific dietary preferences and production needs in the cheese industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL) market is divided into two key types: Intermittent and Continuous Malfunction Indicator Lamps. Intermittent MILs temporarily activate to signal issues that may not be persistent, often indicating transient problems in vehicle systems. In contrast, Continuous MILs remain illuminated when a fault is detected, signifying more serious or persistent issues. Both types serve crucial roles in vehicle diagnostics, enhancing driver awareness of potential engine problems and ensuring timely maintenance to avoid severe damage.</p></p>

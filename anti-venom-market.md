<p><p>The anti-venom market is divided into two primary types: polyvalent and monovalent anti-venom. Polyvalent anti-venom is designed to treat multiple species of venomous snakes, making it versatile and useful in regions with varied snake populations. In contrast, monovalent anti-venom targets a specific species, offering a more tailored treatment, which can improve efficacy and reduce potential side effects. Both types play crucial roles in snakebite management, with their use depending on the local snake fauna and treatment protocols.</p></p>

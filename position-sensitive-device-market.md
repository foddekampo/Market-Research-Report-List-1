<p><strong>The Position Sensitive Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Dimensional</li><li>Two Dimensional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Position Sensitive Device market is categorized into One-Dimensional and Two-Dimensional types based on the spatial detection capabilities. One-Dimensional devices measure position along a single axis, primarily tracking linear movements. In contrast, Two-Dimensional devices can detect positions across two axes, allowing for more complex spatial measurements. One-Dimensional devices are commonly used in applications like linear encoders, whereas Two-Dimensional devices find use in touchscreen technologies and optical sensors, offering broader applications in automation and user interaction.</p></p>

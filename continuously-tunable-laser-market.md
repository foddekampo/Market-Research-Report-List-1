<p><p>The continuously tunable laser market can be categorized into three types: solid, gas, and liquid lasers. Solid lasers utilize a solid-state medium, such as crystals or glass, providing high efficiency and stability for various applications. Gas lasers employ gaseous mediums, offering high output power and tunability; they are suitable for applications like spectroscopy. Liquid lasers use dye solutions that allow for a wide range of wavelengths, making them ideal for research and specialized tasks. Each type serves unique applications in industries like telecommunications, medical, and scientific research.</p></p>

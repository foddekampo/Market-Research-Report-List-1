<p><p>The thermoelectric devices market is categorized into three main types: bulk thermoelectric devices, micro thermoelectric devices, and thin-film thermoelectric devices. Bulk thermoelectric devices are typically larger and used for applications requiring significant power generation or cooling, such as power generation from waste heat. Micro thermoelectric devices are smaller and suitable for miniaturized applications like wearable electronics. Thin-film thermoelectric devices are characterized by their lightweight and flexible nature, making them ideal for integrating into advanced materials and electronics, providing efficient energy conversion in compact spaces.</p></p>

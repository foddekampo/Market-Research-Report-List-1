<p><p>The aluminum brazing sheets market encompasses various applications, including torch brazing, induction brazing, and furnace brazing. Torch brazing involves using a handheld torch to heat the aluminum and filler metal, suitable for small-scale operations. Induction brazing utilizes electromagnetic induction for precise heating, ideal for automated production lines. Furnace brazing employs high-temperature controlled environments for large-scale applications, ensuring uniform heating and optimal bonding. Together, these methods cater to diverse industries, including automotive, HVAC, and electronics, emphasizing efficiency and strength in joining aluminum parts.</p></p>

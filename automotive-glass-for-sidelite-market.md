<p><p>The automotive glass sidelite market consists of various types, primarily including tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, commonly used in side windows. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers bonded with an interlayer, offering improved UV protection, noise reduction, and resistance to shattering, making it ideal for higher-end vehicles. Other types may include coated or tinted options, catering to aesthetic preferences and additional functionalities, addressing diverse consumer needs in the automotive sector.</p></p>

Vertical Type probe cards feature vertical orientation, enabling multi-site testing and reducing space requirements. MEMS Type probe cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems for enhanced performance and scalability, allowing for advanced testing capabilities. Each type caters to different testing needs in semiconductor manufacturing and development.</p></p>

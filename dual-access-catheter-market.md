<p><p>The Dual Access Catheter market is categorized into two main types based on the design of radiopaque markers: "Two Distal Radiopaque Markers Spaced" and "Single Radiopaque Marker at Distal Tip." The former features two distinct markers positioned along the catheter's length, enhancing visibility under imaging for precise placement and navigation. In contrast, the latter design incorporates a single marker located at the distal tip, simplifying visualization for practitioners. Both designs aim to optimize catheter performance in various medical procedures.</p></p>

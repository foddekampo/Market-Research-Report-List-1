<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Line Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The line isolator market is categorized into three main types: bus side, line side, and transfer bus side. </p><p>The bus side isolators are placed between the bus and the outgoing circuit, ensuring safe maintenance. Line side isolators isolate incoming power lines from the system for repairs or adjustments. Transfer bus side isolators connect multiple bus systems, allowing for seamless switching and maintenance without service interruptions. Each type ensures reliability and safety in electrical systems, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>

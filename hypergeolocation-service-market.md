<p><strong>The Hypergeolocation Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPP</li><li>RTK</li><li>RTK-PPP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hypergeolocation Service market comprises three main types: PPP (Precise Point Positioning), RTK (Real-Time Kinematic), and RTK-PPP (Real-Time Kinematic with Precise Point Positioning). </p><p>PPP offers accurate positioning using satellite data independently, ideal for applications needing high precision without a reference station. RTK enhances GPS accuracy through real-time corrections from a nearby base station, suitable for dynamic applications like surveying. RTK-PPP combines both technologies, ensuring real-time accuracy while maintaining flexibility and robustness, particularly in challenging environments.</p></p>

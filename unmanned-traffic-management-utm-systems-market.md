<p><p>Unmanned Traffic Management (UTM) systems are categorized into two main market types: non-persistent UTM and persistent UTM. In contrast, persistent UTM systems provide continuous surveillance and management of airspace, ensuring safety and efficiency for regular drone operations. These systems support various applications, enabling scalable integration of UAVs into national airspace.</p></p>

<p><p>The flash point testers market is primarily segmented into open cup and closed cup testers. Open cup flash point testers measure the temperature at which a substance's vapor ignites when exposed to an open flame, typically used for high flash point liquids. In contrast, closed cup testers determine the flash point in a sealed environment, providing more accurate readings for low flash point liquids. Both types are essential for assessing the flammability and safety of various materials in industries such as chemicals and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generator</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generator market comprises primarily two types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) nitrogen generators and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA generators utilize adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from air under pressure, providing high-purity nitrogen suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, membrane nitrogen generators employ selective permeation through hollow fibers to separate nitrogen, offering a simpler and more compact solution. Both technologies cater to diverse sectors, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and electronics, each with specific purity and flow rate requirements.</p></p>

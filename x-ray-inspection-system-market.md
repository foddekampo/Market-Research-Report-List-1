<p><p>The X-ray Inspection System market is categorized into film-based and digital imaging types. Film-based imaging utilizes traditional photographic films to capture X-ray images, offering high resolution but requiring development time and careful handling. In contrast, digital imaging employs advanced sensors and software to create instant, high-quality images, enhancing efficiency and analysis. Digital systems allow for easier storage, retrieval, and sharing of images, making them increasingly popular in industries such as security, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electronic Multiplier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic multiplier market consists of various types, primarily Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) and Continuous-Dynode Multipliers. CEMs are designed for high-sensitivity applications, using discrete channels to amplify electron signals, making them ideal for photon counting and mass spectroscopy. Continuous-dynode multipliers, on the other hand, utilize a continuous surface to exponentially amplify electrons, offering higher gains and faster response times. Both types serve critical roles in scientific research, analytical instrumentation, and medical diagnostics, driving innovations in detection technologies.</p></p>

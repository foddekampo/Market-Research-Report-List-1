<p><strong>The Automotive Motor Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO)</li><li>CRGO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive motor lamination market features two primary types: Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO) and Cold Rolled Grain Oriented (CRGO) steel. CRNO laminations are used for applications requiring general magnetic performance, offering isotropic magnetic properties and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, CRGO laminations are specifically engineered for high efficiency, providing superior magnetic properties in a specific direction, which is crucial for electric motors and transformers. Both types play vital roles in enhancing the performance and efficiency of automotive electric powertrains.</p></p>

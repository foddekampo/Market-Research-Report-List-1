<p><p>The Peripherally Inserted Central Catheter (PICC) market is primarily segmented into two types: Power Injected Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters and Conventional Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters. Power Injected PICCs are designed to withstand high-pressure injections, making them ideal for imaging procedures like CT scans, where contrast media is essential. Conversely, Conventional PICCs are used for standard intravenous therapy and medication administration, providing a less specialized option for longer-term venous access. Both types serve critical roles in patient care.</p></p>

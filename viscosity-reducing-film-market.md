<p><p>The viscosity reducing film market is primarily divided into two types: UV viscosity reducing film and thermal viscosity reducing film. UV viscosity reducing films utilize ultraviolet light to decrease the viscosity of liquids, facilitating easier handling and application in various industries. In contrast, thermal viscosity reducing films operate through heat application, which alters the fluid's flow properties for improved performance in processing and transportation. Both types enhance efficiency in fluid management across sectors such as chemicals, oil, and manufacturing.</p></p>

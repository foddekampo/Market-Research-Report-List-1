<p><p>Impeller pumps are available in various materials, each offering unique advantages. Neoprene impellers are known for their resilience against wear and chemicals, making them suitable for corrosive environments. Polyurethane impellers provide excellent abrasion resistance and flexibility, ideal for handling slurries. Stainless steel impellers offer superior strength and durability, particularly in high-pressure applications. Bronze impellers have good corrosion resistance and are often used in marine environments. Additionally, "other" materials may include specialty composites or elastomers tailored for specific applications and conditions.</p></p>

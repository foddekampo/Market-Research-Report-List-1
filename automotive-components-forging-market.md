<p><strong>The Automotive Components Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive components forging market comprises three main types: closed die forgings, open die forgings, and rolled rings forgings. Closed die forgings involve shaping metal in precision molds, ensuring high accuracy and complex geometries for critical parts. Open die forgings use flat dies to shape larger workpieces, allowing for simpler designs and versatility. Rolled rings forgings involve creating ring-like components, often used in gears and bearing applications, providing strength and durability essential for automotive performance. Each type caters to specific automotive needs.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Electric Power Steering (EPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering device market features three primary types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which uses hydraulic fluids to assist steering; Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), combining hydraulic and electric components for enhanced efficiency and control; and Electric Power Steering (EPS), which relies solely on electric motors for precise steering without hydraulic fluids. Each type offers distinct advantages in responsiveness, fuel efficiency, and weight savings, catering to varying automotive designs and consumer preferences, thereby shaping the future of vehicle steering systems.</p></p>

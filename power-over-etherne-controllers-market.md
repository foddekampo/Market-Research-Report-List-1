<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) controllers are essential for managing power delivery across Ethernet networks. The market involves two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and Power Device (PD) controllers. PSE controllers are integrated into network devices, delivering power to connected devices, like VoIP phones and cameras, while PD controllers are embedded in end devices, managing the power received. Both play a critical role in enabling efficient power distribution, simplifying installation, and enhancing device functionality within networked environments.</p></p>

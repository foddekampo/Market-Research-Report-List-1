<p><strong>The Injection Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Servo Robot</li><li>Pneumatic Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection robot market comprises primarily two types: servo robots and pneumatic robots. Servo robots utilize electric motors for precise control and high-speed operation, making them ideal for complex molding tasks with tight tolerances. In contrast, pneumatic robots leverage compressed air for movement, offering simplicity and lower costs, though they may lack the precision of servo systems. Each type serves specific applications in industries like automotive and consumer goods, optimizing production efficiency and enhancing automation in injection molding processes.</p></p>

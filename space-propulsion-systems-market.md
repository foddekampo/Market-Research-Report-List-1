<p><strong>The Space Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Fuel Chemical Propulsion</li><li>Liquid Fuel Chemical Propulsion</li><li>Cold-Gas Chemical Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Space propulsion systems are categorized mainly into three types based on the fuel used. Solid fuel chemical propulsion utilizes a pre-composed solid propellant, offering simplicity and reliability for launch vehicles. Liquid fuel chemical propulsion involves liquid propellants that allow for adjustable thrust and higher efficiency, commonly used in rockets. Cold-gas chemical propulsion operates using inert gas expelled from a tank, providing low thrust with minimal complexity, mainly employed for fine attitude control and small satellite maneuvers.</p></p>

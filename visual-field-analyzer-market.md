<p><p>A Visual Field Analyzer is a diagnostic tool used primarily in ophthalmology to measure a patient's peripheral vision and assess the integrity of the visual field. The device typically utilizes techniques like static perimetry and kinetic perimetry to provide detailed maps of a patient's visual field.</p><p>The Visual Field Analyzer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.60% during the forecast period.

<p><ul><li>With Antibiogram</li><li>MALDI-TOF</li><li>Gram Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bacterial identification system market comprises various techniques for diagnosing bacterial infections. Antibiogram testing determines bacterial sensitivity to antibiotics, guiding effective treatment. MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight) is a rapid, highly accurate method that identifies bacteria based on their protein profiles. Gram staining is a traditional technique that classifies bacteria into Gram-positive or Gram-negative, providing preliminary information on their nature. Together, these systems enhance pathogen identification and contribute to better clinical outcomes by enabling targeted antibiotic therapy.</p></p>

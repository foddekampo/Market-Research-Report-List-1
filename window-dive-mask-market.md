<p><p>The Window Dive Mask market is categorized into two main types: Full Window Dive Masks and Half Window Dive Masks. Full Window Dive Masks feature a large, single pane of tempered glass, providing an unobstructed field of vision and enhanced panoramic views underwater, ideal for divers seeking immersive experiences. Conversely, Half Window Dive Masks have a smaller, dual-lens design that may limit visibility but often offers improved fit and comfort. Both types are essential for divers, catering to different preferences and diving conditions.</p></p>

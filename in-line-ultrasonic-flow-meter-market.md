<p><p>In-Line Ultrasonic Flow Meters are categorized based on their measurement paths. Single/Dual Path meters utilize one or two sets of transducers to measure flow, suited for applications requiring simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Multi-Path meters, featuring three or more transducer pairs, provide enhanced accuracy and reliability by measuring flow from multiple angles, minimizing the impact of factors like turbulence. These types are essential for various industries, ensuring precise fluid measurement in diverse conditions and enhancing overall system performance.</p></p>

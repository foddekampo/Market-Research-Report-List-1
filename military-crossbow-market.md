<p><p>The military crossbow market comprises three main types: compound crossbows, recurve crossbows, and others. Compound crossbows utilize a system of pulleys and cables to enhance power and accuracy, making them suitable for tactical use. Recurve crossbows feature a simpler design with limbs that curve away from the shooter, offering traditional performance and ease of maintenance. The "others" category includes specialized crossbows designed for unique military applications, incorporating advanced materials and technology to meet specific operational needs.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Optical Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Gratings</li><li>Transmission Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical gratings market comprises primarily two types: reflective gratings and transmission gratings. Reflective gratings utilize surface reflections to disperse light, making them suitable for applications in telescopes and spectrometers. Transmission gratings, on the other hand, allow light to pass through while diffracting it, commonly used in laboratory experiments and optical devices. Both types play a crucial role in wavelength separation and manipulation, serving diverse industries, including telecommunications, metrology, and spectroscopy, by optimizing optical performance.</p></p>

<p><p>The plastic cable cleat market comprises three primary types: single type, multicore type, and trefoil type. Single type cleats support individual cables, ensuring secure placement and minimal movement. Multicore type cleats accommodate multiple cables bundled together, providing enhanced organization and protection. Trefoil type cleats are designed for three-phase power cables, maintaining equal spacing and support to optimize performance and reduce electromagnetic interference. Each type caters to specific installation needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in electrical applications.</p></p>

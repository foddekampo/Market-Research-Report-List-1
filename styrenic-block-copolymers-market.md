SBS is known for its excellent elasticity and compatibility with various materials, making it ideal for applications like adhesives and sealants. SIS offers superior flexibility and durability, often used in medical and automotive sectors. HSBC provides enhanced thermal and UV stability, commonly utilized in roofing, coatings, and asphalt modification, ensuring versatile performance across diverse industries.</p></p>

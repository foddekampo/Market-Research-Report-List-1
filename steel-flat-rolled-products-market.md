<p><p>The steel flat-rolled products market consists primarily of cold rolled coils and hot rolled coils, along with other specialized products. Cold rolled coils are produced through the process of rolling steel at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface and tighter tolerances, making them ideal for applications in automotive and appliances. Hot rolled coils, on the other hand, are manufactured at elevated temperatures, leading to reduced production costs and increased malleability; they are used in construction and heavy machinery. Other products may include galvanized or coated steel.</p></p>
